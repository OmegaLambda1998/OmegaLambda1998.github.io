Patrick Armstrong (He/Him)

I am a PhD student at the Australian National University, with a background in physics and computing. I am currently studying how to improve systematic uncertainties within dark energy cosmology via simulating and forward modelling the systematics. I am also an active maintainer of the cosmological pipeline Pippin, and a member of the Dark Energy Survey, where I participate in a number of other cosmological projects.
